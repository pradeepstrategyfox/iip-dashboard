Dear Engineering Professional,

Thank you for expressing interest in advancing your career in the packaging industry. You are now one step closer to earning one of India's most respected professional credentials.

The Certified Packaging Engineer (CPE) programme, offered by the Indian Institute of Packaging, Chennai, an autonomous body under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India, is now accepting applications for Batch VI (2026-2027).

With the global packaging industry projected to reach $125 billion by 2030 in India alone, credentialed packaging engineers are in unprecedented demand across FMCG, pharmaceuticals, food processing, logistics, and e-commerce.

Packaging Design & Informatics • Glass, Metal & Wood Packaging • Cellulose & Sustainable Materials • Polymer Science & Mould Engineering • Materials Testing Practical

S2 Semester II: Specialisation November 2026 - February 2027
